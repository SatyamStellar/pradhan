Who is Geetanjali Jakhmola?

Grassroots Leader, Not Just a Figurehead

Geetanjali Jakhmola is the elected Gram Pradhan of Haripur Kalan, known for her transparent leadership, action-oriented governance, and deep connection with the people.

She has led multiple initiatives across sanitation, water management, women empowerment, and infrastructure that directly impacted villagers' lives.
